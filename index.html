I’m a public health scientist with over a decade of experience leading and supporting evidence-based work that shapes health strategy, strengthens services, and tackles health inequalities. I currently provide population health intelligence and strategic analysis for local government in the UK..

I’ve worked across contrasting health data contexts in both high- and lower-resource settings. That blend gives me a practical edge in preparing multi-year datasets, improving data quality, and translating imperfect routine data into credible insights that can inform strategy and investment

This portfolio documents my journey through the "Generative AI for Work & Research" course, where I am applying cutting-edge AI tools to enhance research productivity and automate complex public health workflows.

About The Course

An 8-week live cohort course by The GRAPH Courses, focusing on No-Code AI Tools for Responsible Task Automation.

Module 1

Websites & Docs

Generating Markdown/Latex responsibly, building research websites, and designing presentations with AI.

Module 2

AI Data Analysis

Using ChatGPT Code Interpreter, Julius, and Windsurf to process datasets and create automated reports.

Module 3

Text Analysis

Literature reviews with Elicit/LiteRev, text classification, and sentiment analysis for research.

Module 4

Custom Chatbots

Building RAG systems, custom GPTs, and interactive chatbots with Botpress.

Module 5

Task Automation

Designing AI agents for literature monitoring and orchestrating app-to-app workflows.

Module 6

Communication

AI-generated research summaries, video abstracts, and scientific visualization.

Module 7

Capstone

Final project showcase demonstrating responsible AI practice in a public health context.
